Ingredients (for 4 burgers)

For the patties

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat is best)
  • 1 small onion (grated or finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)

For the burger sauce (optional but tasty)

  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp vinegar or pickle juice
  • ½ tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Burger assembly:

  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce (iceberg or romaine)
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (red or white)
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices (cheddar or American)
  • Butter (for toasting buns)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or any sauce you like
